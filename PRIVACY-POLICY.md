Privacy Policy for SolaGameCube

Last updated: February 22, 2026

SolaGameCube is a mobile gaming application built for the Solana ecosystem.

1. Information We Collect
- Solana wallet address (when you connect a wallet such as Phantom, Solflare, or Seeker)
- Gameplay data (points earned, games played, session time) – stored locally on device and optionally on-chain via Solana
- No personally identifiable information (name, email, phone) is collected unless voluntarily provided.

2. How We Use Information
- To enable gameplay, point tracking, and future SKR token redemption
- To display anonymous leaderboards
- To improve app performance and features

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties
- We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:
  - As required by law
  - With service providers (e.g., Solana blockchain for on-chain interactions)
- Wallet connections are handled directly via Mobile Wallet Adapter – we do not store private keys.

4. Security
- We use industry-standard practices to protect data transmitted to/from the app.
- Blockchain interactions are public by nature (Solana network transparency).

5. Your Rights
- You can disconnect your wallet at any time.
- No data is stored off-device except on-chain public records.

6. Changes to This Policy
- We may update this policy. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of changes.
